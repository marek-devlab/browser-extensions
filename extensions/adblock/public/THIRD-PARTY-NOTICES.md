## Filter list data — GPL-3.0 / CC-BY-SA

The blocking rules in `rules/*.json` are **data**, not executable code: they are
declarative match patterns that the browser's own engine evaluates. They are derived from
AdGuard's converted DNR rulesets, which in turn derive from EasyList and EasyPrivacy.

See [`rules/ATTRIBUTION.md`](./rules/ATTRIBUTION.md), which ships alongside the rule data,
for the exact per-list provenance, upstream projects, ruleset version, and license terms.
